Hi, I'm Grayson, and I'm passionate about helping society address climate change.

Right now, I lead research at CarbonPlan to ensure the scientific integrity of the global carbon market. My work has exposed hundreds of millions of dollars in bad offset credits.

Previously, I spent roughly a decade in academia studying forests and plant physiology. I spent most of that time staring at leaves and processing terabytes of satellite imagery in an effort to improve how we measure photosynthesis at the global scale.

Before that, I was the first data scientist at Lyft and worked on some of our first dispatching algorithms.
